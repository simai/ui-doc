# Breakpoint descriptions

* **sm (small):** From 576px. Use for small devices such as phones.
* **md (medium):** From 768px. Suits tablets.
* **lg (large):** From 960px. For larger tablets and small desktops.
* **xl (extra large):** From 1152px. For mid to large desktops.
* **xxl (extra extra large):** From 1536px. For wide monitors.
