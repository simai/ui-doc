# Breakpoint descriptions

* **sm (small):** for viewports starting at 576px. Use it to tune styles for small devices such as phones.
* **md (medium):** from 768px, suited for tablets.
* **lg (large):** kicks in at 960px. Apply it for larger tablets and compact desktops.
* **xl (extra large):** from 1152px; this breakpoint targets mid-size and large desktop screens.
* **xxl (extra extra large):** for very wide displays beginning at 1536px, ideal for large monitors and ultra-wide screens.
