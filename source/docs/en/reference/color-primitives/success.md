# Success

![][image17]

The **Success** role is intended for visually representing states that signal a successfully completed process or an
achieved goal. The Success color scheme helps users notice a positive result and feel confident when interacting with
the interface.

Success role variations:

* **Success** — fills, text, and icons with a strong accent indicating a successful result.
* **On Success** — text and icons optimized for readability on the Success color background.
* **Success Container** — muted fill used for navigation elements, tonal buttons, and other objects associated with
  successful states.
* **On Success Container** — text and icons on the Success Container background.
* **On Success Container Graphic** — fills of large graphic objects on the Success Container background.
* **Success Transparent** — a semi-transparent color for highlighting interactive elements with a transparent background
  on hover (for example, for outline buttons).
* **Outline Success** — color for outlines, borders, or dividers associated with successful states.
